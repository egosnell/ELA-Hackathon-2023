ABOUT ACAP SAINT JOHN

ACAP Saint John is a community based monitoring program leading the way in restoring and preserving Saint Johns local aquatic habitats. ACAP was founded in 1991 under Environment Canada in line with goals to preserve the Atlantic Canadian coastal environment.

ACAP specializes in water quality monitoring, educational programs, habitat preservation and community engagement in environmental issues. Goals To engage multi-sectorial community of Greater Saint John in collaborative management and restoration of our watersheds ACAP Saint John envisions a sustainable community that embraces that inter-dependence of the unique social, economic and environmental characteristics of the regions watershed This tool meets these goals by connecting local community with powerful data relavant to their local landscape. We hope to inspire community to engage themselves with and understand changes happening in their watershed. Encouraging community members to understand this data renders them stakeholders in the solution.
