THE PROBLEM ACAP FACES

ACAP Saint John is a community based monitoring program leading the way in restoring and preserving Saint Johns local aquatic habitats. ACAP was founded in 1991 under Environment Canada in line with goals to preserve the Atlantic Canadian coastal environment. ACAP specializes in water quality monitoring, educational programs, habitat preservation and community engagement in environmental issues.

GOALS

1. To engage multi-sectorial community of Greater Saint John in collaborative management and restoration of our watersheds

2. ACAP Saint John envisions a sustainable community that embraces that inter-dependence of the unique social, economic and environmental characteristics of the regions watershed
